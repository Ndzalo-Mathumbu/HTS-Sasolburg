      Mission
    • To cultivate future engineers, innovators, and problem-solvers by providing a strong foundation in technical and academic education.
    • To equip learners with practical skills and critical thinking abilities that prepare them for careers in engineering, technology, and other competitive industries.
    • To uphold Christian values while fostering integrity, discipline, and respect in all aspects of school life.
    • To create a balanced learning environment that promotes both academic excellence and extracurricular achievement in sports, culture, and leadership.
    • To stay at the forefront of technical education by adapting to new technologies, industry demands, and global advancements.
    Vision

    Our vision at HTS Sasolburg is to be the leading technical high school in the Free State, recognized for producing skilled, innovative, and responsible graduates who excel in both academics and industry. We aim to inspire every learner to reach their highest potential by fostering a culture of curiosity, creativity, and continuous improvement. Through advanced technical training, strong academic foundations, and values-based education, we strive to prepare our students to become the engineers, leaders, and change-makers of tomorrow — ready to shape the future with integrity and excellence.

    Values

    • Innovation — We embrace creativity, curiosity, and the courage to solve problems in new ways.
    • Excellence — We set and pursue high standards in academics, technical skill, and personal growth.
    • Integrity — We act with honesty, fairness, and accountability.
    • Respect — We treat one another with dignity, listen actively, and value diverse perspectives.
    • Teamwork — We collaborate effectively, support each other, and achieve more together.
    • Faith — We uphold Christian values that guide our decisions and inspire compassionate leadership.
